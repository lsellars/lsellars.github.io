Youth Employment 

The importance of youth employment 
Work Ethic

Work ethic is very important to anyone. The quicker teens get a job the sooner they learn to have work ethic. Work ethic carries you through life and provides motivation. 

Financial Responsibility 

Financial responsibility is transformed into youth through employment. It gives them the opportunity to buy things and save money. A job when you are young can set you up for financial stability. 

Time Management 

Youth employment provides time management skills. It makes you balance your time with school, home, and extra curricular activities. It also requires time managment wihtin the job and teaches showing up on time.
